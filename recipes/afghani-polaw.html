Kabab of Taza Gul

Description

This is the best polaw of KP. which is known as Peshawari Afghan Polaw.


Ingredients

  • Qeema
  • Flour
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Egg
  • Masala
  • Mirch

Steps

  1. First put the Qeema in large Plate.
  2. Throw flour powder on it.
  3. Cut Tomatoes,Green Mirch and union into small pieces.
  4. Mix the Ingredients and left for some time.
  5. Make small parts of mixture.
  6. Then throw it in boiling Oil and eat it.
